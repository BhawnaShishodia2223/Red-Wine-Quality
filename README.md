<h3 align="center">About Dataset</h3>

- <h4><b>Input variables :</b></h4>

1 - fixed acidity

2 - volatile acidity

3 - citric acid

4 - residual sugar

5 - chlorides

6 - free sulfur dioxide

7 - total sulfur dioxide

8 - density

9 - pH

10 - sulphates

11 - alcohol

- <h4><b>Output variable :</b></h4>

12 - quality (score between 0 and 10)
